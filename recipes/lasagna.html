Lasagna

A delicious Lasagna!

This is a very common and yet delicious dish, a fan favorite of myself!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
